Im sure everyone knows what a job is and im sure everyone knows why they are important too. But many people are unsure of how to get a job when they get to that point in their lives. I know it can be quite confusing. However we are here to help you out. As you continue to browse this page you will learn the essential tools you will need in order to get the job that you desire.

Linkedin is a very useful resource to get yourself out there. What is linkedin you may ask? Linkedin is a social media platform however it is used stricly for promoting yourself professionally. Linkedin is NOT a resume A good Linkedin profile includes: A profesional profile photo, your education, your experiences, volunteer experience, your skills, as well as a personal summary of yourself. The personal summary should include a little bit abotu who you are as a person in addition to your work life.

A well put together resume is probably the most important thing when looking for a job. A resume will allow for you to share your past acomplishments skill and training. As well as your educational background. A good resume includes: A header with contact information such as your linkedin, a section about you past experiences, your education, and a section about your skills.
